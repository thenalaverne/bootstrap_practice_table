Kiki is a girl who had fled to the city by her parents as she is in the stage where she is already independent. When Kiki got a job at her new town, she met a boy and evetually fell in love with eachother Her ability is to fly with a brrom, that's why the title says "Delivery Service" because she applied for a job where she delivers bread to houses using her superpower.

Tombo has no ability, but I guess he had the power to make Kiki fall in love with him. I guess he had one decent ability, to make a flying plane by hand.
